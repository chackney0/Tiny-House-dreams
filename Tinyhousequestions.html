When Deciding to move into a tiny house what questions should you be asking yourself?

  • What size house Do you want yo live in?
  • What color do you want your house to be?
  • Do you want stairs, a ladder or one story?
  • How will you get heat or air?
  • How will you shower and go to the bathroom?
  • Where do you want to live
    • Will you be mobile?
    • Or do you want to stay in one place?
  • Where are you going to sleep?
    • How big of a bed do you want?
    • How much head room do you want?
    • Do you want to climb stairs/ladder every time?
  • How will you prepare food?
  • Are you prepared to live in this tiny space?
    • What about storage?
    • Closet Space....
